Dairy Milk Classic

"A masterpiece of taste and texture"

The iconic Dairy Milk Classic is a symphony of the finest ingredients: a whole roasted hazelnut nestled in a bed of smooth hazelnut cream, wrapped in a delicate crispy wafer, and coated in exquisite milk chocolate. Each piece is individually wrapped in golden foil, making it a luxurious treat for yourself or a perfect gift.
